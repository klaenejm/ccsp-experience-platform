Status Report to Illyria:
While we have been a target of state and non-state actors alike, our main focus is on the Kingdom of Illyria. Illirya has employed against us multiple spear-phishing attacks, which are often part of their broader campaigns. Additionally, our assets have been targets of denial of service (DoS) and distributed denial of service (DDoS) attacks. We cannot confirm attribution to Illyria, but prepositioned malware in our critical infrastructure has been found. Overall, we are responding to a broad range of Illyrian hostile activities in cyberspace, including, but not limited to those listed above.
